== Description ==
SocialParts.com is the best platform to grow your website traffic, build email list, increase conversions, and bring more sales for online businesses.
Here’s what SocialParts.com can do for you:
 * Get more website traffic with dynamic social share buttons
 * Increase visitor engagement by displaying live, customized messages
 * Bring more sales by utilizing “Social Proof” effect 
 * Create social proof to show page visitors, recent signups, recent purchases and more
 * SocialParts.com is lightweight and built to scale, so it won’t slow down your site MOBILE friendly! Choose how your notifications are displayed on mobile, or turn them off for mobile devices
 * Hide SocialParts.com from specific pages
 * Loop notifications
 * Customize the message, style, position and timing to suit your branding.
 * Support your language!
 * See more at [SocialParts.com](https://socialparts.com)
Note: this plugin is using [SocialParts.com](https://socialparts.com) 3rd party service for collecting statistics and managing displaying widgets parameters,
in order to find out what data is transmitted to external service, you can follow the link to a [Privacy Policy](https://socialparts.com/privacy-policy)
and also check services [terms of use](https://socialparts.com/terms).


== Installation ==
1. Upload folder social-parts.zip to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
1. Activate the plugin through the Plugins menu in WordPress
1. Login through Social Parts admin page
1. Enjoy this
